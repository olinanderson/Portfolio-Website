About me

I'm passionate about innovation, design, and AI/machine learning. I have a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and completed a 16 month co-op in Project Governance at TC Energy. I'm currently working on converting a van into a full-fledged RV.

Regarding web development, I've built extensive personal projects which have included some of the following technologies: MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, HTML, CSS, JQuery, Socket.io, several graphing libraries, etc. I'm proficient with SPAs (single page applications) - state management and the overall architecture surrounding how front-end frameworks work. I'm extremely comfortable with creating beautiful UI and responsive applications, as well as being an excellent problem solver.

Regarding other skills/passions, I have many personal projects under my belt, some of which include: designing and building a reliable large-scale cartesian style 3D printer (all of which was designed in Solidworks before hand), programmed several full-fledged websites and ideas (using MERN stack, MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), and currently designing and building a camper from an empty cargo van shell. The camper van includes several systems working together to create a working home - the battery bank and electrical system, the water system with recirculating shower, and the control system for the electric ball valves within the water system, with everything designed fully in Solidworks.
